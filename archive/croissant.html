Croissant en Costume

Croissant en Costume
French guy in Cologne
Has to drink, kiss and change costumes
Get the highscore











Jean-Jacques-Gerard is a french guy who visites cologne during the carnival. Now a bar tender shows him the rituals of cologne carnival and poor Jaques Jean Gerrard has to fullfill them all.
Special thanks to the famous cologne bands Kasalla and KUHL and the Gaeng for their great music.
